[ FRANKOTE SERIES ]

CERAMIC COATINGS

// Product Description

Ceramic-based protective coatings — both liquid and dry — engineered to prevent molten metal adhesion on ladles, thermocouple tubes, runners, and ferrous surfaces. Water-based formulations with controlled additives ensure smooth application and consistent film thickness.

Dry variants ship water-blended at the customer site for cold-weather convenience, eliminating concerns about freeze damage during transport and storage. The Frankote line was developed specifically for applications where conventional coatings fail under sustained thermal cycling.

Boron nitride variants (BN400 series) deliver non-wetting performance in demanding environments where standard ceramic formulations reach their limits — particularly in aluminum refining applications with high superheat temperatures.

02
[ FRANLUBE SERIES ]

DIE RELEASE LUBRICANTS

// Product Description

A diversified line of water-extendable die release lubricants for casting aluminum, magnesium, and zinc alloys. The Franlube series covers the full range of production requirements — from single-cavity prototype tooling to high-volume multi-cavity production dies.

High-temperature formulations address the specific challenges of dies with elevated thermal loads, where standard lubricants carbonize prematurely or fail to maintain consistent film integrity through the full shot cycle. Cosmetic-grade variants meet the surface finish requirements of visible components.

The newest additions to the series feature ceramic-based formulations engineered for exotic aluminum alloys and high-heat producing dies — applications where the chemistry demands exceed conventional water-extendable products. 03
[ FRANLUBE SHOT SERIES ]

SHOT TIP LUBRICANTS

// Product Description

Dry, oil-based, and water-cleanable shot tip lubricants designed to enhance lubrication through the metal injection process while extending tip life. The Franlube Shot series addresses one of the most wear-intensive interfaces in the die casting cell — the shot sleeve and plunger tip.

Ceramic-based dry formulations significantly reduce fire and smoke at the shot end, improving operator environment and reducing visible emissions without compromising lubrication performance. This is particularly relevant for operations with air quality monitoring or operations moving toward cleaner production standards.

Water-cleanable formulations simplify maintenance procedures and reduce downtime associated with tip and sleeve cleaning between production runs.

04
[ SPECIALTY LINE ]

SPECIALTY PRODUCTS

// Product Description

Purpose-built compounds for the specific challenges of die casting operations that fall outside the standard lubricant and coating categories. This line addresses the ancillary chemistry needs of a complete casting cell.

Anti-solder wax (ASW-80) removes metal deposits from die cavity surfaces without damaging tool steel — a recurring maintenance challenge on production dies that see extended runs. The flux (KAB 124) is blended for efficient cleaning with reduced refractory erosion, serving refining and metal treatment operations.

Ejector pin compound (#38) provides high-temperature protection for pins operating in extreme thermal and mechanical environments. These products are sold directly to foundry engineers who know what they need — contact us if you have a specific challenge that doesn't fit a standard product category.
