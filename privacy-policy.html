Overview

LaFrance Manufacturing ("we," "us," or "our") operates this website to provide information about our products and services. This policy explains how we handle information collected through this site. We do not sell products directly through this website, and we do not maintain user accounts or store payment data.

By using this site, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you have questions, contact us directly using the information at the bottom of this page.

02 /

Information We Collect

We only collect information you voluntarily provide. This occurs through the contact form on our Contact page. The form collects:

  • Your name
  • Company or facility name (optional)
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Email address
  • Area of interest (product line)
  • Message content

We do not collect information passively beyond what is described in the Cookies & Analytics section below.

03 /

How We Use It

Information submitted through the contact form is used solely to respond to your inquiry. Specifically:

  • To respond to product or technical questions
  • To route your inquiry to the appropriate technical contact
  • To follow up on open inquiries

We do not use your contact information for marketing campaigns, newsletters, or automated outreach. We do not share or sell your information to third parties.

04 /

Cookies & Analytics

This website uses localStorage to remember your language and display theme preferences (light/dark mode) between visits. This data is stored locally in your browser and is never transmitted to our servers.

We do not currently use third-party analytics, advertising cookies, or tracking pixels. If this changes, this policy will be updated and the effective date revised.

05 /

Third Parties

This site loads fonts from Google Fonts. By visiting this site, your browser may send a request to Google's servers as part of loading font files. Google's privacy policy governs that interaction: policies.google.com/privacy.

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect certain technical data (such as IP addresses) as part of standard server operations. See GitHub's privacy statement for details.

We do not embed social media widgets, advertising networks, or other third-party scripts.

06 /

Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry or to maintain a business relationship. If you would like your contact information removed from our records, contact us at the address below and we will accommodate the request promptly.

07 /

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of personal information we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, contact us directly. We do not require a specific form or process — a plain email is sufficient.

Because we do not use marketing databases or automated processing, most requests can be fulfilled immediately.
