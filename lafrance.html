Our Advantage

60 YEARS
OF RIGHT
CHEMISTRY

LaFrance Manufacturing has been formulating specialty products for metal casting operations since the 1960s. We built our business on a simple premise: the right chemistry, the right people, and the right systems for the job.

We were early pioneers in water-extendable synthetic lubricants — developing our first commercially viable water-based formulations during the oil crisis of the late 1970s when the industry had to evolve. Our Frankote ceramic coating series brought non-wetting, high-temperature protection to ladles and furnace surfaces at a time when alternatives fell short.

Today, our formulations serve aluminum, brass, copper, magnesium, and zinc operations across the globe. We ship worldwide and work directly with your team to identify the right product for your specific alloy, die geometry, and production conditions.

When a new casting challenge comes in, our answer is chemistry — not a search through a distributor's catalog. That distinction is why foundry engineers have been calling us directly for six decades.
